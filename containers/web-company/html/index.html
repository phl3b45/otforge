About Us

Built on 26 Years of Field Experience

Meridian Process Controls was founded in 1998 by instrumentation engineers from the refining and water-treatment sectors. Today we operate from offices in Houston, TX and Rotterdam, NL, supporting critical infrastructure customers across North America, Europe, and the Gulf region.

Our engineering team maintains vendor-neutral expertise across every major DCS and PLC platform. All remote access sessions are logged and audited in accordance with NERC CIP and IEC 62443.
